This circular route through many of the Yorkshire dales was devised by Mark Reid.  We followed the suggestions in his book "The Inn Way to the Yorkshire Dales" as to stopping places, and we found the book useful in many ways.  Besides notes on route-finding and sketch maps, it has information about villages and sights and history along the way.
